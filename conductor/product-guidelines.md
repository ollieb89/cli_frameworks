# Communication Style

## Tone and Voice
- **User-Selectable Personalities:** The CLI must offer a choice of personalities (e.g., Witty & Engaging vs. Crisp & Technical) to suit the user's current working style or mood.
- **Witty & Engaging (Default):** The default tone should be conversational, expressive, and high-energy, perfectly aligning with the "vibe-coding" aesthetic. It should use emojis and maintain a helpful, partner-like presence.
- **Empowering Messaging:** Every interaction should focus on empowering the user, emphasizing their agency and the "superpowers" the AI provides.

## Error and Warning Handling
- **Graceful and Explanatory:** Errors should never be silent or cryptic. The CLI must provide clear, contextual explanations of what went wrong and, where possible, offer actionable suggested fixes.
- **Honest Feedback:** Build trust by being transparent about limitations, potential risks, and the reasoning behind AI-generated decisions.

- **Empowerment:** The suite is designed to amplify the user's capabilities.
